Bio

Colin Burgess is a front end web developer in Boston. After beginning his education in graphic design at the Community College of Vermont he made the move from Burlington, VT down to Boston and attained his BFA in Illustration from Lesley University’s College of Art and Design. His passion for digital art and fascination with technology led him to consider the field of web development. Its unique combination of both technical and creative challenges inspired him to expand his design skill set to the field of web development. He chose to attend Launch Academy’s immersive full stack programming bootcamp to help kickstart his new career ambitions. In his free time he likes to explore other creative outlets like drawing, painting and cooking and likes to spend time outdoors observing wildlife. He is continually working on new projects and researching new tools and technologies to keep learning and to see where the field is going in the future.
